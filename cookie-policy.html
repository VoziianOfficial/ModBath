      What cookies are

      Cookies are small files or pieces of information that may be stored on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may include pixels, tags, scripts, local storage, session storage, and analytics identifiers.

      These technologies may help websites function, remember preferences, understand how visitors use pages, support security, and improve the overall site experience.

      How cookies may be used

      ModBath may use cookies and similar technologies to operate the website, remember cookie consent choices, improve navigation, understand site performance, analyze visitor behavior, prevent misuse, and support request-related functionality.

      Cookies may also help ModBath understand which bathroom remodeling categories visitors interact with, such as full bathroom remodeling, shower remodeling, bathtub replacement, tub-to-shower conversion, bathroom tile and flooring, or vanity and cabinet updates.

      Types of cookies

      The website may use essential cookies that are needed for basic operation, analytics cookies that help measure site performance, preference cookies that remember user choices, and security-related technologies that help protect the website from misuse.

      Some cookies may be session cookies that expire when the browser closes. Others may be persistent cookies that remain for a period of time unless deleted by the user or the browser.

      Third-party technologies

      ModBath may work with third-party service providers that use cookies or similar technologies to provide analytics, hosting, security, communication tools, embedded features, or other website functionality.

      Third-party providers may process information according to their own privacy and cookie practices. ModBath does not control third-party cookie settings or policies.

      Your cookie choices

      You may be able to manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow users to block, delete, or limit cookies. Some site features may not work as intended if cookies are disabled.

      Where a cookie banner or consent tool is available, you may use it to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Essential cookies may still be used because they support basic site functionality and security.

      Do Not Track signals

      Some browsers may send “Do Not Track” signals. Because standards for responding to these signals are not uniform, this website may not respond to all such signals.

      Updates to this Cookie Policy

      ModBath may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The effective date near the top of this page indicates when this policy was last updated.
