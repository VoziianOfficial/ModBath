      Privacy Policy

      This Privacy Policy explains how ModBath may collect, use, share, and protect information when homeowners use this site to start bathroom remodeling requests.

      Effective date: January 1, 2026 Privacy notice

      Information clarity for homeowners.

      ModBath is a request platform, not a bathroom remodeling contractor. This page explains privacy practices connected to site use and request submissions.

      Information we may collect

      ModBath may collect information that you provide directly through this website, including your name, email address, phone number, city, ZIP code, project type, and message details about your bathroom remodeling request.

      The site may also collect basic technical information such as browser type, device information, pages viewed, approximate location derived from technical data, referral information, and interaction data. This information may be collected through cookies, analytics tools, server logs, or similar technologies.

      How information may be used

      Information may be used to process and organize your request, communicate with you, improve website functionality, understand site performance, maintain security, prevent misuse, and support service matching.

      ModBath may use submitted project information to help identify relevant bathroom remodeling categories, such as full bathroom remodeling, shower remodeling, bathtub replacement, tub-to-shower conversion, tile and flooring, or vanity and cabinet updates.

      How information may be shared

      ModBath may share information with independent service providers, matching partners, technology vendors, analytics providers, communication tools, legal advisors, or other service providers that help operate this website and process homeowner requests.

      Independent providers are not employees of ModBath. Any provider contacted through or after using this site is responsible for its own licensing, insurance, estimates, communications, work, pricing, and service performance.

      Your privacy choices

      You may choose not to submit a request through this website. You may also contact ModBath to ask questions about your information or to request updates to communication preferences where applicable.

      Browser settings may allow you to block or delete cookies. Some site features may not function as intended if cookies or similar technologies are disabled.

      Security and retention

      ModBath may use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. However, no method of online transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

      Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the website, process requests, maintain business records, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and improve services.

      Children’s privacy

      This website is intended for adults who are interested in bathroom remodeling services. ModBath does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

      Updates to this policy

      ModBath may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date near the top of this page indicates when this policy was last updated.
